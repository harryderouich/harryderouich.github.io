Addictive Merchandise

Addictive Merchandise are a well established promotional merchandise distributor serving customers across the country. Wanting something more managable than their legacy PHP site, I undertook the creation of a Wordpress/WooCommerce based product catalog.

As part of the process, Addictive Merchandise had to ingest a large number of data feeds from partner factories, containing product ranges and their specification. The cleansing and standardising of data was included in my work for this project.

Features

  • Over 1,300 products published with quantity based pricing matrix.
  • Straightforward quotation request system.
  • Additional information and contact pages.

Further tasks as outlined above included data manipulation and preparation of provided images.
